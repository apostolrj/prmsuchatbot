About Us

Welcome to our chatbot! The researchers, students of President Ramon Magsaysay State University under the College of Communication and Information Technology developed a chatbot to create an exceptional conversational experience. Our chatbot is designed to provide intelligent, personalized, and efficient interactions, delivering information and assistance in a user-friendly manner. Here are the researchers:

  • Ronald Joseph Apostol
  • Basim Sami Fathallah
  • Jude Vincent Sudweste
  • John Ed Romanban

The objective of a chatbot is to provide automated assistance and engage in conversation with users in a way that simulates human-like interaction. Chatbots are designed to understand user queries, provide relevant information, answer questions, and perform tasks based on predefined rules, patterns, or artificial intelligence algorithms.

Services

Timely Information

Our chatbot offers quick and instant answers to the user's queries. This helps users get the information or assistance they need without unnecessary delays.

Answering your Queries

Some of your questions about the University's general information that can be made public will be addressed by the chatbot. Such as, admission, mission, and vision and many more.

Automated Assistance

Automated chatbots can work around the clock, providing support at all hours of the day. This is especially useful in situations where human agents might not be available, including after business hours or during periods of high traffic.
